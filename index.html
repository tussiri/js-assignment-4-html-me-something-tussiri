Arusha

These are pictures of a couple of the interesting and culturally significant places you can visit in Arusha. Starting with Lake Duluti, just outside Arusha. The lake formed after a volcanic eruption. I took this picture while on a canoe on a 2019 trip to Tanzania with my mother. The other three pictures were taken at the Arusha Cultural Heritage Center, which houses thousands of artifacts & paintings from different parts of Tanzania.
